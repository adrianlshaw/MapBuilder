Mapbuilder

Developed alongside BhamNav, this application creates custom maps in the form of KML files, which can be used in the BhamNav application to create other university maps.

Welcome to MapBuilder.

MapBuilder is a tool for creating maps for Varsity (commercially known as BhamNav). The reason that the tool was created was to provide an easy way for people to plot their own maps for their universities. Simply open the Android app, hop on your bike and cycle round each path. You may do this by foot, but it can be very tiring, as I have found out!
